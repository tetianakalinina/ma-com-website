About the programme

MA COM prepares graduates for leadership roles in public, corporate and institutional communication. You will practise audience research, content strategy, data‑informed decision‑making and campaign design across digital and offline media.

  • Hands‑on labs and seminars with practitioners
  • Opportunities for internships and research projects
  • Strong links with Milan’s media and corporate ecosystem
